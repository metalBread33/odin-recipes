Chicken Tender Sub Order

Description

The Publix chicken tender sub is an iconic sandwhich here in florida. As someone who works in the Publix deli where this sandwhich is made, I know some of the best ways to prepare this sub and some of the wost. I will give y'all my prefered way to make this sub.

Ingredients

  • Good attitude
  • Pleasent smile
  • Don't be rude for the love of god I'm just doing my job

Instructions

  1. Ask for the sub of wheat bread
  2. Ask for American cheese (white and yello taste the same so it doesn't matter)
  3. Get it toasted
  4. Add buffalo sauce and ranch
