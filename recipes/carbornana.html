Carbonara

Description

Carbonana is a classic italian pasta dish that is super easy to make. Its crispy bits of pork, peppery bite and rich cheese sauce make it a staple in every cook's recipe book. With the right substitutions, it can even be made on a budget!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb thick cut bacon
  • 3 whole eggs
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 4 oz parmesan
  • 2 cloves of garlic (optional)
  • 16 oz pasta
  • Ground black pepper
  • Kosher salt

Instructions

  1. Cut bacon into pieces that are a little larger than bite sized (they will shrink while they are cooking.)
  2. Place bacon onto a cold pan. Set the pan on medium heat and cook until bacon is done. Optionally, add garlic to pan and let it cook for about 30 seconds.
  3. Once bacon is done cooking, strain most of the grease leaving about 2 tablespoons left in the pan
  4. Combine eggs and egg yolks in a bowl
  5. Add cheese and pepper to egg mixture, stir with a fork until homogenous
  6. Add pasta to a pot, add water, and cook accoring to the box instructions
  7. Save about 1/3 cup of starchy pasta water. Drain pasta.
  8. Add pasta and then egg-cheese mixture to the pan with bacon. (If need be, add bacon and mixture to the pot of pasta.)
  9. Add pasta water until desired consistency
